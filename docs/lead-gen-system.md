# Lead Generation System

## Objective
Identify, qualify, and convert high-value business leads into paying clients.

---

## Lead Sources
- Google Maps scraping
- Instagram business profiles
- Etsy sellers
- Facebook Marketplace
- Local directories

---

## Lead Qualification Model

Each lead is scored (1–10):

### Score Criteria
- Website quality (0–3)
- Online presence (0–3)
- Business demand level (0–2)
- Responsiveness likelihood (0–2)

---

## High Value Lead Definition
Score 7+ = high priority outreach

---

## Output Format
- Name
- Business type
- Contact info
- Website
- Lead score
- Notes

---

## Monetization
Leads can be:
- Used for direct client outreach
- Packaged and sold
- Used for internal funnels
